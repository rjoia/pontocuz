How it works

From photo to pattern
in minutes

01
Upload an image

Choose any photo from your phone or computer. The app supports PNG, JPG, and GIF files.

02
Crop and choose the size

Select the part you want to stitch and decide how many stitches your project will have.

03
Choose your DMC palette

The app picks the best colours automatically. You can fine-tune brightness and contrast too.

04
Start stitching with your guide

Use Stitch Mode to see a clean pattern while you work, one stitch at a time.
